About Me

Hello, my name is Katie. I’m a graduate of Michigan State University with a Bachelor’s degree in Advertising with a concentration in Social Media. While at MSU my biggest accomplishment, besides earning my degree, was getting Spartan Stadium accessible to all students. To do this I used Facebook to bring awareness to the issue and keep everyone updated throughout the two year process. Many alumni and fans sent messages of support while sharing their MSU experiences with me. Currently, I am a Freelance Digital Marketing Specialist.
